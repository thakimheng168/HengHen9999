History of Western University

Western University was officially founded by Sub-Decree No. 72 on 17 November 2003 by its founder, Mr. Te Laurent. In the same year, Mr. Laurent also established the Western International School. By 2005, with approval from the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport, a branch of Western University was opened in Kompong Cham province.

Mr. Laurent, the Rector of Western University, graduated in Economic Science specializing in Finance and Banking from the University of Sorbonne, Paris, France. Since its establishment, the University has continually strengthened its executive structure and academic processes to provide high-quality education that supports Cambodia’s human resource development.

To achieve this mission, Western University has embarked on programs to further improve the quality of teaching and align its standards with international benchmarks.
