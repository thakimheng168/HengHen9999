Vision, Mission & Values

Western University was founded by Sub-Decree No. 72 on 17 November 2003 by its founder, Mr. Te Laurent, who also established the Western International School in the same year. In 2005, with approval from the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport, a branch of Western University was opened in Kompong Cham province. Mr. Laurent, the Rector of Western University, graduated in Economic Science specializing in Finance and Banking from the University of Sorbonne, Paris, France. Since its establishment, the University has continually strengthened its executive structure and academic processes to provide high-quality education that supports Cambodia’s human resource development. To achieve this mission, Western University has embarked on programs to further improve the quality of teaching and align its standards with international benchmarks.

Vision

To develop a bright future for people to become leaders with creativity, social conscience, and responsibility.

Mission

In adherence to its motto, “Where Quality Comes First”, Western University is committed to providing quality education, strengthening research and development, promoting entrepreneurship, and upholding human values for sustainable development.

Goals

  • Strengthen Governance, Management, and Finance
  • Improve Quality of Teaching and Learning
  • Increase Community Services
  • Develop Human Resources
  • Promote Research & Development and Entrepreneurship
  • Expand Learning and Physical Resources
